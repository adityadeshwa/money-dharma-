# 💰 MoneyDharma

MoneyDharma is a **financial literacy web application** designed to educate beginners, youth, and new investors about essential financial concepts. The platform provides **audio and video learning content** in **Hindi and English** to make financial education accessible and easy to understand.

The goal of MoneyDharma is to help users become aware of **financial frauds, government schemes, investment funds, and smart money management practices**.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🎥 **Audio & Video Learning** – Easy-to-understand financial lessons
- 🌐 **Bilingual Support** – Available in **Hindi and English**
- 🌙 **Light & Dark Mode** – Better accessibility and user experience
- 🔐 **User Authentication** – Secure login and signup using **Firebase Authentication**
- 📚 **Beginner Friendly Content** – Designed for beginners and young learners
- ⚠️ **Fraud Awareness** – Educates users about financial scams and fraud prevention
- 💡 **Financial Knowledge** – Covers topics like funds, schemes, and money management
